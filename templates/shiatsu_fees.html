Price Increases

I'm increasing the standard cost of an hour-long session to £55.

I'm not changing my concessionary rate or Shiatsu student rate which remain at £35 as I dislike cost being a barrier to entry.

Appointment Fees:

  • Full sessions last one hour - £55
  • Shiatsu Student rate - £35
  • Concessionary rates - £35

Reduced rates for courses of treatments, payment to be made at start of course.

  • £150 for 3 sessions
  • £200 for 4 sessions
  • £250 for 5 sessions
  • £300 for 6 sessions

Cancellation Policy

Please note: Appointments cancelled with less than 48 hours notice will incur a cancellation fee of £25 to cover lost appointment time.

Want £10 off a Session? Try my Referral Scheme

After any session with me you can request (or I'll just give you) some referral cards. You can then give these to anyone you feel would like a go. When they hand the voucher in after their 1st treatment I'll take £10 off for them and for your next visit too! They will however only apply to full price sessions. This is a great way to introduce new people to the benefits of Shiatsu and keep the price down for yourself!
